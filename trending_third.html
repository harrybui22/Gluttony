Cool combos of food!

by sportsarecool on 2/26/2014

If you're a bacon enthusiast like me, you're always on the lookout for food items containing delicious sizzling bacon. I tried the grilled cheese open faced melt which was beautifully sprinkled with bacon (could use a little more) and Cheetos puffs right on top. The Melt Combo allows you to get a melt of your choice and a 16oz. rocks drink, which isn't too bad compared. I also tried the Naked Angel Ice-T, which was pretty good. You could actually taste and SEE the fresh fruits in the drink, (drink is garnished with a cute slice of orange.) It was just the right amount of sweet. Would definitely return again, but lines do get pretty long so expect a long wait.
