Definitely coming back!

by Steven P on 2/21/2013

A friend and I doubled down for the Meal for 2 option and were completely stoked with the outcome. The lamb stew-esc curry was tender and the vegetable curry, almost korma style, was out of this world. Combine that with the best naan I have had in SD, unreal traditional potato samosas, REAL Tandoor Chicken, and some truly flavorful sweet and herby chutneys; What do you Get? ME, w/a HUGE smile, coming back for more ASAP, as often as possible, and spreading the word of this places Quality!

Beyond my Expectation

by Jenny Z on 8/13/2012

For a person that doesn't eat Indian food that often, I thought the place should offer some translation of their menu. Took me the longest time to find out Naan was this delicious bread!! The cashier was also a little reluctant to help due to the long line. I suggest everyone to do their research before they come here!

Got the Chicken Biryani for $8.95 to go. They surrrre know how to fill a styrofoam box! Definitely enough for that one order to be split amongst two people. Very very flavorful and the best I've ever had.
