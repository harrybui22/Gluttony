Just like home

by ilovefood on 12/9/2012

My usual staple here is #37, the rice with grilled pork and meatloaf. Its really good, and there is always more than enough and I end up taking half of it with me. Last time we were here, on Thursday, I decided to be different and try one of their Banh Mi sandwiches....O....M...G!!! It was SOOO good!! Wow I got the Pork Roll Deluxe, and when it came I was skeptical, but WOW the flavor was amazing!! Everything on the sandwich was so good and combined for a wonderful, unique taste....and of course the bread was really great, super soft and flavorful! I'm pretty sure every time I come here now I'm getting one of the sandwiches!

Great broth!

by brothlover on 10/16/2012

Great customer service from these folks but I am sorry to say this but the food is ewwww! Seeing that I am Vietnamese, I would say I have pretty good taste buds for viet food and this place puts food to shame! I went here twice because of the convenient location and because I was pretty hungry and desperate and each time it was a disappointment because the lack of flavors in all of their foods! Each time I would say I had amazing customer service though! Prices are higher than most viet restaurants (still cheap) but higher! Go down the street a bit and you will find a much better viet place that (for this area) is my go to for pho and viet cravings.
