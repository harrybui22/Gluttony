I'm overwhelmed by the quality

by kbbqmaster on 3/24/2013

The PRIME CUT of BEEF is a MUST at this fine Korean BBQ Establishment!! I was visiting the San Diego area and my friend and I wanted to enjoy some kbbq. We happen upon Manna and it was the best decision that was have made! There are 2 options for dinner: $19.99 for dinner A or $24.99 for dinner B. If you just want to satisfy a crave for kbbq, go for dinner A. However, if you want to eat prime cuts of meat and really enjoy your dinner, go to dinner B! You will not forget your experience here!

Everything is amazing

by meatman on 12/2/2012

SO. MUCH. FOOD. STOMACH FILLED TO MAXIMUM CAPACITY. NOT HUNGRY FOR REST OF WEEKEND. T'was delicious. Only complaint is that the marinated beef we got was a little too salty. Ate here Saturday afternoon, came around opening time, no wait. Also, parking here is easy (big parking lot)! Woot.

Too good

by joeyboy on 5/23/2012

Kind of pricey, meat quality isn't that good, service is great if you're Korean! But I've seen non-Koreans struggle here. Going with the cheaper option here is going to cost you about $27/person after tax and tip. I honestly think the best things they have here are the brisket and beef tongue. The other meats just aren't good - either not marinated correctly, cuts of meat are wrong or quality just sucks.
