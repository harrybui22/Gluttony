Best ramen ever!

by ramenlover on 8/2/2013

I feel like ramen shops should be fast, easy, and delicious. I love coming here because it's simple and straightforward, no nonsense, I-will-drink-all-this-goddamn-delicious-broth ramen. There's times I literally can't wait for anyone, so I'll go alone because I just NEED this ramen in my life. The broth is so flavorful -- a little on the heavier side maybe, but well worth it. My friends have tried the side dishes and mentioned they're nothing special. The ramen is really the star of the show here.

Never tasted such flavorful broth

by iloveramentoo on 7/24/2013

I ordered the Tonkatsu Kotteri which is the ramen with the black garlic oil. The ramen came with a slice of pork and half an egg! I really enjoyed the bowl of ramen immensely. The standard bowl is a small portion though - perfect for a girl with a normal appetite, but if a guy is ordering, there might not be enough food so I suggest ordering an extra portion or sides!

New favorite ramen joint

by ultimateasianfood on 5/10/2013

I'm assuming the only thing keeping this place from having 5 stars is their pricing, which is fair, but a little expensive for your typical frugal student like myself. The first time i ate here i ordered Spicy Tonkotsu (spicy 3) with Yamadaya toppings and extra noodles, and it was one of my most memorable food experiences in recent times. Even my girlfriend noticed how extremely happy i was eating that bowl of delicious magic. The second time around i learned to leave the pork behind for later- attack the broth first and then dig in to the noodles and the rest. I am by no means a ramen connoisseur, but i'm awaiting the day i try something better. I just don't see it coming.
