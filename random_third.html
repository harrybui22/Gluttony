A HIDDEN GEM

by meatlover on 6/24/2013

Places like this are why I love Yelp. While shopping in the area and craving a burger we went onto yelp, found this place highly rated and decided to try it. Never would have known of the place otherwise and would have missed out on a fantastic meal. We ordered the chopped bacon burger with tater tots and the avocado bacon swiss burger with sweet potato fries. The burgers were huge, loved that they were stuffed with cheese. Both burgers were juicy and delicious.

by burgersrule on 4/10/2013

I think of this place as a luxurious indulgence, that sinful treat you have maybe once every few months. The burger I had was amazing. Phenomenal. It was 20 minutes of a deeply intimate, messy, and satisfying experience.
